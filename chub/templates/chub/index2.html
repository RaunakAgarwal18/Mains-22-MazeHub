RULES AND REGULATION
  • There will be 4 separate levels in this hunt. Try to hunt as much as you can.
    • Level 1 : Fangs of Death and Hell
    • Level 2 : Secret in the Shadows
    • Level 3 : Realm of Mystery
    • Level 4 : Lure of the Present
  • The official hunt was held live on 5th September 2021, but now we are releasing it for anyone to attempt at any time. Modifications include :
    • You can go back to previous levels which you have solved.
    • There are Detailed hint and Answer options added in case you feel very clueless go for the Detailed hint, but it mostly contains dead-giveaways so it might ruin the experience for you if you use it. We recommend you try as hard as you can without it. This was not there in the original hunt. Also, in the original hunt, a penalty of 20 minutes was added for every hint accessed.
  • Submit the answer in the form which is shown at the end of each level. Once you submit, the link for the next level will be displayed. For some levels, finding where the portal is, is also part of the hunt !
  • You are allowed to make as many guesses as you want.
  • Since this was a common doubt during the actual game, we will clarify it here itself - you can include spaces in the answers if required. For level 1, the answer is case-insensitive.
  • Participants are free to use the internet and other resources to solve the levels.
